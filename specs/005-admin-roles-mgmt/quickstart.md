# Quickstart: Admin Roles Management

## Prerequisites
- Logged in as an Administrator.

## Navigation
1.  Open the VibeCRM application.
2.  In the left sidebar, locate the **Setup** section.
3.  Click **Setup** to expand the menu.
4.  Click **Roles** to access the Role Management page.

## Managing Roles

### Creating a Role
1.  On the Roles page, click the **New Role** button.
2.  Enter the **Label** (e.g., "Sales Manager").
3.  Enter the **API Name** (e.g., "sales_manager"). This must be unique, lowercase, and contain no spaces.
4.  (Optional) Enter a **Description**.
5.  Click **Create**.

### Viewing/Editing a Role
1.  Click on any role in the list (or the "View" icon).
2.  Review the role details.
3.  To update permissions, edit the permissions JSON field (coming soon: simplified UI).
4.  Click **Save** to apply changes.

### Deleting a Role
1.  Locate the role in the list.
2.  Click the **Delete** (trash bin) icon.
3.  Confirm the deletion.
    *   *Note: System roles cannot be deleted.*
